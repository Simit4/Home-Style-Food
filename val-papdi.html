Val Papdi Curry

A classic Nepalese‑style curry with flat beans, warmly spiced and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1–2 green chillies, chopped
  • 2 small tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 medium potato, diced
  • 250g valor beans (val papdi), trimmed & chopped
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • ¼ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1½ tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan and temper cumin and fenugreek seeds until they sputter.
  2. Add garlic, green chillies, and onion; sauté until golden.
  3. Add diced potato with a pinch of salt; cook for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Stir in val papdi, turmeric powder, and a little salt; mix well.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes; cook until they soften and release juices.
  6. Sprinkle in coriander powder, red chilli powder, and garam masala.
  7. Cover and simmer on low heat until beans and potatoes are tender and oil separates.
  8. Serve hot with roti, rice, or your favorite flatbread.
