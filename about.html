About Home Style Food

Welcome to Home Style Food — a simple platform where we share home-cooked, delicious, and easy-to-follow recipes. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced cook, our dishes are made with love, inspired by everyday Nepali and global home cooking.

The recipes featured here are cooked and curated by Simit and his wife, sharing family favorites that bring warmth and comfort to every table. We focus on real ingredients, simple methods, and bold flavors.

Thank you for visiting and happy cooking!
