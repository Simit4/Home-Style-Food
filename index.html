Veg Chowmein Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 🧅 1 Onion – sliced
  • 🌶️ 2 Green chillies – chopped
  • 🫑 ½ Green capsicum – sliced
  • 🥬 1 cup Cabbage – shredded
  • 🍜 150g Chowmein noodles – boiled
  • 🌶️ 1 tsp Chilli powder
  • 🥄 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 🧂 Salt – to taste
  • 🛢️ 2 tbsp Oil
  • 🧂 A pinch of MSG (optional)
