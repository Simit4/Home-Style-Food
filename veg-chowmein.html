Easy Veg Chowmein

Ingredients:

  • 200g noodles
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Boil noodles until cooked, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and stir fry the vegetables.
  3. Add noodles and soy sauce. Mix well.
  4. Cook for 2–3 minutes. Serve hot.
