Easy Veg Chowmein

A quick and flavorful Nepalese-style vegetarian noodle dish.

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, capsicum, cabbage)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)

Method

  1. Boil the noodles as per packet instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or pan. Add garlic and stir-fry for a few seconds.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry on high heat for 2‚Äì3 minutes.
  4. Add boiled noodles, soy sauce, chili sauce, salt, and pepper.
  5. Toss everything well for another 2 minutes.
  6. Serve hot, optionally with ketchup or achar.
