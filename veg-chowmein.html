Easy Veg Chowmein

A quick and flavorful Nepalese‑style vegetarian noodle dish.

🛒 Ingredients Used:

  • 🧅 1 Onion – sliced
  • 🌶️ 2 Green chillies – chopped
  • 🫑 ½ Green capsicum – sliced
  • 🥬 1 cup Cabbage – shredded
  • 🍜 150g Chowmein noodles – boiled & strained
  • 🌶️ 1 tsp Chilli powder
  • 🧂 1 tsp Chowmein masala
  • 🧂 Salt – to taste
  • 🥄 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • ✨ MSG – optional
  • 🛢️ 2 tbsp Oil
  • 🍅 Tomato ketchup – to serve

👨‍🍳 Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok on high heat.
  2. Add sliced onion and green chillies. Stir-fry for 1 minute.
  3. Add capsicum and cabbage. Stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add the boiled noodles and toss well.
  5. Sprinkle chilli powder, chowmein masala, salt, and a pinch of MSG (optional).
  6. Pour soy sauce and mix everything nicely on high heat.
  7. Stir-fry for another 1–2 minutes until everything is well combined.
  8. Serve hot with some tomato ketchup on the side.
